About Us

Empowering Vulnerable Communities in Kenya Through Education, Health & Support

Open Arms Project is committed to uplifting vulnerable communities across Kenya by combining compassion with practical support. In cities such as Nairobi and Kisumu, we provide access to digital literacy, educational resources, and essential services that empower individuals to build better futures. Our mission is rooted in humanity — extending a helping hand where it is most needed and ensuring that no one is left behind

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities in Kenya by providing access to education, healthcare, and digital literacy, ensuring that every individual has the opportunity to build a sustainable and dignified future.

Our Vision

A Kenya where no one is left behind — where compassion, technology, and education unite to create inclusive communities that thrive with opportunity, dignity, and hope.

Khalif Ochieng , Founder

Open Arms Project was founded by Khalif Ochieng, whose experience in plant operations and tourism management shaped his vision of combining practical skills with compassion. Driven by a passion for community empowerment, Khalif established the project to extend a helping hand to vulnerable groups in Kenya, ensuring that technology, education, and humanity work together to change lives.
